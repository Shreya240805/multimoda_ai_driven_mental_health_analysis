📋 Assessment Guidelines

Before You Begin

Please read these guidelines carefully to ensure the best assessment experience. This will take approximately 5-7 minutes.

🎥 Technical Requirements

  • Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browser for best results
  • Ensure your webcam and microphone are working properly
  • Close other applications that might use your camera or microphone
  • Use headphones (recommended) to avoid audio feedback

💡 Environment Setup

  • Good lighting: Face a window or light source so your face is clearly visible
  • Quiet space: Choose a quiet room to minimize background noise
  • Stable position: Sit comfortably and keep your face centered in the camera frame
  • Privacy: Ensure you're in a private space where you can speak freely

🎤 How the Assessment Works

  • The system will ask you 9 questions about your mental health (PHQ-9)
  • Each question will be read aloud by the system
  • Answer each question verbally in your own words - speak naturally
  • Your facial expressions, voice tone, and words will be analyzed in real-time
  • Click "Stop Speaking" when you finish answering each question
  • You can skip questions if needed, but complete answers provide better insights

⌚ Optional: Cardiovascular Data (Smartwatch)

  • If you have a Samsung smartwatch, Apple Watch, or fitness tracker, you can upload heart rate data
  • This is completely optional but provides additional insights
  • After completing the assessment, you'll have the option to upload a CSV file

📱 To export heart rate data from Samsung Health:
1. Open Samsung Health app
2. Tap Menu (☰) → Settings
3. Tap "Download personal data"
4. Select "Heart rate" data
5. Choose date range: Last 2 weeks
6. Download the CSV file to your phone/computer

🍎 To export from Apple Health:
1. Open Health app
2. Tap your profile picture → Export All Health Data
3. Extract the exported ZIP file
4. Find "HeartRate.csv" or use third-party apps like "Health Export CSV"

📊 What We Analyze

  • Facial Emotions: Happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, fear, neutral expressions
  • Voice Patterns: Tone, pitch, energy, vocal emotions
  • Speech Content: Keywords, sentiment (positive/negative words), language patterns
  • Cardiovascular Data (if uploaded): Heart rate variability, stress indicators
⚠️ Important Notes:
• This is a screening tool, not a diagnosis
• Be honest and speak naturally - there are no right or wrong answers
• Your data is confidential and stored securely
• If you feel uncomfortable at any time, you can end the assessment
• The assessment takes approximately 5-7 minutes to complete

🔒 Privacy & Data Security:
Your voice recordings, facial data, and responses are processed in real-time and stored securely in our database. We do not share your personal health information with third parties. You can delete your assessment data at any time from your dashboard.
